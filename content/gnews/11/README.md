##  Opening Up America Again 

##  **重新開放美國** 

President Trump has unveiled Guidelines for Opening Up America Again, a three-phased approach based on the advice of public health experts. These steps will help state and local officials when reopening their economies, getting people back to work, and continuing to protect American lives .

川普總統已經宣布了重新開放美國指導方針，這是個基於公共衛生專家建議的三階段措施。這些步驟將幫助州和地方官員重新開放經濟，讓人們返回工作崗位，並繼續保護美國人民的生命安全。

##  Criteria 

##  **準測** 

###  **Proposed State or Regional Gating Criteria** 

###  **擬議州或地區重新開放准入標準** 

Satisfy Before Proceeding to Phased Comeback

在進入回歸階段前應符合

####  **SYMPTOMS** 

**症狀**

Downward trajectory of influenza-like illnesses (ILI) reported within a 14-day period

AND

14天內類似流感病例報告數據呈下降趨勢，以及

Downward trajectory of covid-like syndromic cases reported within a 14-day period

14天內類似冠狀病毒症狀病例報告數據呈下降趨勢

####  **CASES** 

####  **病例** 

Downward trajectory of documented cases within a 14-day period

OR

14天內在案病例呈下降趨勢，或者

Downward trajectory of positive tests as a percent of total tests within a 14-day period (flat or increasing volume of tests)

14天內陽性檢測在總檢測比例中呈下降趨勢（平緩或檢測總量增加）

####  **HOSPITALS** 

####  **醫院** 

Treat all patients without crisis care AND

有救治所有無需危機護理的患者的能力

Robust testing program in place for at-risk healthcare workers, including emerging antibody testing

有對處於高風險醫護人員的強大檢測系統，包括緊急抗體檢測

State and local officials may need to tailor the application of these criteria to local circumstances (eg, metropolitan areas that have suffered severe COVID outbreaks, rural and suburban areas where outbreaks have not occurred or have been mild). Additionally, where appropriate, Governors should work on a regional basis to satisfy these criteria and to progress through the phases outlined below.

州和地方官員可能需要根據當地具體情況酌情使用這些標準（比如，已經發生冠狀病毒爆發的大城市聚集區，鄉村或城市遠郊未出現感染或感染不嚴重的地方）。另外，如適用，州長應該以地區為基礎來努力達到準則要求從而進展到以下所述的各階段。

###  **各州核心準備職責** 

####  TESTING & CONTACT TRACING 

#### 檢測&接觸史跟踪

- Ability to quickly set up safe and efficient screening and testing sites for symptomatic individuals and trace contacts of COVID+ results

- Ability to test Syndromic/ILI-indicated persons for COVID and trace contacts of COVID+ results

- Ensure sentinel surveillance sites are screening for asymptomatic cases and contacts for COVID+ results are traced (sites operate at locations that serve older individuals, lower-income Americans, racial minorities, and Native Americans)


- 確保對前哨監控點進行無症狀病例篩查，並對冠狀病毒檢測結果陽性者接觸史的跟踪（這些篩查點設立在專門為老年人，低收入的美國人，少數族裔和美洲原住民服務的區域）

#### 衛生保健系統能力

- 能夠快速獨立地供給充足的個人防護設備和關鍵醫療器械，以應對激增的需求
- Ability to surge ICU capacity
- 能夠提高緊急救護病房（ICU)容量

- 保護關鍵行業人員的健康和安全
- Protect the health and safety of those living and working in high-risk facilities (eg, senior care facilities)

- 避免觸碰面部

- Disinfect frequently used items and surfaces as much as possible.
- 盡可能多的對常使用的物品和表面進行消毒
- Strongly consider using face coverings while in public, and particularly when using mass transit.
- 強烈建議在公共場合使用面部遮蓋物品，特別是在乘坐公共交通工具時

##  Phase One 

## 第一階段

For States and Regions that satisfy the gating criteria

針對符合重新開放准入標準的州和地區

###  INDIVIDUALS 

### 個人

[ALL VULNERABLE INDIVIDUALS](https://www.whitehouse.gov/openingamerica/#vulnerable-individuals) should continue to shelter in place. Members of households with vulnerable residents should be aware that by returning to work or other environments where distancing is not practical, they could carry the virus back home. Precautions should be taken to isolate from vulnerable residents .

所有易感染個人必須繼續得到安置。家庭成員中有易感染者的人應當明白，如果他們返回工作崗位或去到其他不能保持距離的地方，他們可能會將病毒帶回家。應該相應的預防措施來隔離易感染者。

All individuals, WHEN IN PUBLIC (eg, parks, outdoor recreation areas, shopping areas), should maximize physical distance from others. Social settings of more than 10 people, where appropriate distancing may not be practical, should be avoided unless precautionary measures are observed .

所有個人， **當在公共場合時** （例如，公園，戶外娛樂場所，購物區）應該最大限度的與其他人保持距離。當不能保持適當距離時，除非預防措施到位，否則應該避免10人以上的聚會。

Avoid SOCIALIZING in groups of more than 10 people in circumstances that do not readily allow for appropriate physical distancing (eg, receptions, trade shows)

當情況不允許保持合適距離時（比如招待會，展覽會），應避免10人以上的團體聚集。

MINIMIZE NON-ESSENTIAL TRAVEL and adhere to CDC guidelines regarding isolation following travel.

盡量減少不必要的旅行以及嚴格遵循疾病預防控制中心CDC有關出行後的隔離指導。

###  **雇主**

只要可能和可行，公司企業繼續鼓勵員工在家工作

在各階段的返工情況下，如果可能

Close COMMON AREAS where personnel are likely to congregate and interact, or enforce strict social distancing protocols.

Minimize NON-ESSENTIAL TRAVEL and adhere to CDC guidelines regarding isolation following travel.

盡量減少不必要的出行以及嚴格遵循疾病預防控制中心CDC有關出行後的隔離指導。

Strongly consider SPECIAL ACCOMMODATIONS for personnel who are members of a VULNERABLE POPULATION.

強烈考慮為易感染人群提供**特殊住宿**

###  SPECIFIC TYPES OF EMPLOYERS 

### 特定類型的雇主

SCHOOLS AND ORGANIZED YOUTH ACTIVITIES (eg, daycare, camp) that are currently closed should remain closed.

學校和有組織的活動（例如日託所，活動營），目前已經關閉的應該繼續保持關閉

VISITS TO SENIOR LIVING FACILITIES AND HOSPITALS should be prohibited. Those who do interact with residents and patients must adhere to strict protocols regarding hygiene.

對養老院和醫院的探視應該被禁止。那些與老人和病人接觸的人員必須遵守嚴格的衛生條例

LARGE VENUES (eg, sit-down dining, movie theaters, sporting venues, places of worship) can operate under strict physical distancing protocols.

大型場所（例如，餐廳，電影院，體育館，宗教禮拜場所）可以在遵守嚴格的保持距離的規定下運營

ELECTIVE SURGERIES can resume, as clinically appropriate, on an outpatient basis at facilities that adhere to CMS guidelines.

在臨床條件允許以及遵照醫療護理和醫療救助服務中心CMS準則的情況下，可以對不需住院的病人進行選擇性手術

GYMS can open if they adhere to strict physical distancing and sanitation protocols.

健身房在遵守嚴格的保持距離和衛生條件規定下可以營業

BARS should remain closed.

酒吧必須保持關閉
